About

Welcome to Comet Study Corner! We connect students and tutors to streamline the learning process!
To start, please sign up for an account if you are a new user or sign into your account if you are a returning user.

Students

Students can search for tutors by name, subject, or availability. Once they find a good fit, students can schedule a meeting, which will show up in their upcoming appointments!

Tutors

Tutors can list their areas of expertise, available hours, and a short blurb about themselves. Once a student schedules a meeting with a tutor, it will appear in the tutor's upcoming appointments!
